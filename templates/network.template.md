Each contact entry follows this format:

```markdown

Notes: Relationship context, what they're working on, how you might help each other, warm intro possibilities
```

**Tips for maintaining your network:**

1. **Update after every interaction** - Even a quick email exchange
2. **Note warm intro paths** - Who knows who
3. **Track what they're working on** - Opportunities to help
4. **Record personal details** - Build genuine relationships
5. **Review weekly** - During your weekly review, scan for follow-up opportunities
